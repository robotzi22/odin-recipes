Raw vegan strawberry pie

Description:

A super easy, raw, vegan strawberry pie! It's absolutely delicious. Feel free to add blueberries also.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup almonds
  • 1 cup shredded coconut
  • 1 cup pitted dates
  • 2 tablespoons coconut oil
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 2 cups strawberries
  • 2 cups shredded coconut
  • 2 tablespoons coconut oil
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 3 strawberries, sliced

Steps for cooking:

  1. Place almonds, 1 cup coconut, dates, 2 tablespoons coconut oil, and salt in a blender. Puree until ingredients form a dough.
  2. Press dough into a pie pan to form the crust.
  3. Place 2 cups strawberries, 2 cups coconut, 2 tablespoons coconut oil, and salt in a blender. Puree into a cream.
  4. Pour cream over the crust. Top with sliced strawberries. Refrigerate until cream is thickened, about 2 hours.
